Friends of Asha Kiran

A team of partly trained palliative volunteers, known as “Friends of Asha Kiran”, from Pampady, Pangada, Meenadom, Kooroppada, Kothala, are also assisting Asha Kiran to identify patients who require palliative support . They schedule and visit the house of these patients, supply the required medical kits etc. They also meet on the 1st of every month to pray for these patients and to discuss the issues they face during the house visits.

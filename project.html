Project

Projects completed. During the centenary celebrations of Pampady Thirumeni on 5th and 6th April, 2015, a Homoeo medical camp was organized inPothenpuram Dayara and dispensed medicines to around 500 patients who were suffering from various illness. Felicitated the children of Ashakiran employees for their achievements in Class ten and Class 12. A Homoeo medical camp was also organized and dispensed medicines to approximately 200 youngsters during the International Conference of Orthodox Youth Movement in Dayara from 14th May to 17th May 2015. Supply of preventive medicines, free of charge, for viral fever, chicken pox, measles, mumps, jaundice, conjunctivitis etc, started from 15th May, 2015.

Upcoming Projects of 2015

Nurses Training July 2015: Two lecturers working in the Gulf Medical University have voluntarily agreed to provide a short term training for our newly passed out nurses on (1) how to face interview (2) review of technical part in nursing (3) basic English language (4) familiarization in Arabic language (5) General Orientation to Nurses (6) An introduction to basic nursing care .It is highlighted that participants will get international exposures by this training free of charge.
Patients get together: In order to get the feedback from patients on the services provided and to rectify in case of any discrepancy in services being provided.
